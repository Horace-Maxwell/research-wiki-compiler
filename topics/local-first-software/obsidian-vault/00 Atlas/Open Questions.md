# Open Questions

## Highest-leverage open questions

- Which sync-engine assumptions are durable enough to keep in canonical pages instead of working notes?
- Which conflict or repair signals should become explicit watchpoints?
- Which migration concerns deserve their own synthesis instead of staying inside a concept page?

## What would reduce uncertainty

- The corpus starts connecting local copies, sync choices, and repair burden in a stable way.
- A context pack can answer migration or watchpoint questions without reopening every source note.
- The same watchpoint keeps changing maintenance order and deserves durable synthesis treatment.

## What might become a synthesis next

- **Local-first sync risk map**: The corpus already hints that sync policy is the real durability boundary, but the practical risk story is still split across the entry page, a concept page, and the maintenance surfaces.
- **Local-first migration playbook**: Migration pressure is already visible as a durable concern, but the corpus is not yet rich enough to turn it into a fully stable operational synthesis.
